Mirrani's Pet Sitting and Boarding

Dog Care

Dogs are “man's best friend” for a reason. They are loyal and loving pack animals that mostly just want to find a happy place in your family. They usually aim to please because that gets them the extra pat or belly rub that they so look forward to as a part of their pack's social behavior. Each breed is different, but they all get lonely when their humans are gone.

When I come to care for your dog, they actually get more than the time that you have paid for because I believe that they should be properly greeted and fussed before I even reach for the leash. Most pet owners do not open the door and immediately send their dog out. They give love and attention to their dog, then continue that interaction into their outdoor time and care. Owners who hire me are guaranteed that their pet receives the full amount of exercise time requested, plus a five to ten minute buffer of introduction and farewell time. No person would want to have a stranger come to their door, pull them to a gym, then drop them back home without sharing some kind of conversation. I don't believe your dog enjoys that either.

If your dog is unable to do much exercise, I remain with them in the home for the full requested time, reading to them or talking to them. It is my firm belief that having an audible presence in the home eases the anxiety of feeling alone and helps with your dog's emotional balance while you are away.
